# User Manual: MyGameVote 🎮

Welcome to **MyGameVote**, the ultimate platform for managing your sports communities and game slots. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Login
- **Sign Up**: Create an account using your email and password.
- **Social Login**: You can also sign up securely on native mobile and web using your **Google** or **Facebook** account.
- **Onboarding**: Every new user must select their preferred sports to customize their experience. 
    - *Note: If you change your sports interests later in your Profile, it generates an "Interest Request" that displays a yellow banner until an Admin approves your new sports.*

## 2. Squads & Organizations

MyGameVote is built for multiple communities. You can belong to one or many "Squads."

### Joining a Squad
- If you have an **Invite Code** (6 characters), enter it on the onboarding screen.
- You can also search for a community by its **Unique Group ID**.
- **Approval**: Once you join via a code, your account will securely lock into an "Approval Pending" screen. A group Admin must approve your profile before you are granted access to the Home Dashboard and game slots.

### Creating a Squad
- Want to start your own community? Tap **"Create your own Squad"**.
- You will become the **Admin** and can immediately start setting up games.

## 3. Playing & Voting

### The Home Dashboard
- View all upcoming games for your active Squad.
- **Voting**: Tap **"JOIN MATCH"** to secure your spot.
- **Waitlist**: If the game is full (usually 14 players), you will be automatically added to the waitlist. If someone leaves, you'll be promoted instantly!

### Payments
- If a game requires a fee, tap **"PAY NOW"** on the game card.
- Select your preferred method (**Zelle** or **PayPal**) and follow the instructions to confirm your payment with the Admin.

## 4. Managing Your Profile

### Changing Sports Interests
To prevent unauthorized access to specific sports events, modifying your sports requires Admin approval.
1. Tap the **Profile / Settings** icon.
2. Under "Your Interests," select or deselect the sports you wish to play.
3. Tap **Save Interests**.
4. **Pending Request**: Your profile will display a yellow banner indicating your request is awaiting Admin review. Once approved, the banner will disappear and your new sport events will unlock!

## 5. Administrative Features (For Squad Owners)

If you are an Admin, you have access to the **Admin Dashboard**:

### Match Setup
- **Regular Match**: Configure your recurring weekly game (e.g., Every Saturday @ 6 PM). The app will automatically generate these games for you.
- **Custom Polls**: Create one-off events for special tournaments or social gatherings.

### Roster Management
- **Approvals**: See a list of users trapped on the "Pending" screen and activate them with one tap using the "OK" button.
- **Pending Interests**: Review and Approve/Reject "Interest Requests" when existing users want to play new sports.
- **Inline Editing**: Use the "Interests" dropdown directly inside the Registered Members list to secretly update a user's sports immediately.
- **Attendance**: View who has joined a match, mark them as paid, or remove them if they can't make it.
