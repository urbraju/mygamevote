# User Manual: MyGameVote 🎮

Welcome to **MyGameVote**, the ultimate platform for managing your sports communities and game slots. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started.

## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Login
- **Sign Up**: Create an account using your email and password.
- **Social Login**: You can also sign up instantly using your **Google** or **Facebook** account.
- **Onboarding**: Every new user must select their preferred sports (e.g., Football, Volleyball, Cricket) to customize their experience.

## 2. Squads & Organizations

MyGameVote is built for multiple communities. You can belong to one or many "Squads."

### Joining a Squad
- If you have an **Invite Code** (6 characters), enter it on the onboarding screen.
- You can also search for a community by its **Unique Group ID**.
- **Approval**: Once you request to join, a group Admin must approve your profile before you can see the game slots.

### Creating a Squad
- Want to start your own community? Tap **"Create your own Squad"**.
- You will become the **Admin** and can immediately start setting up games.

## 3. Playing & Voting

### The Home Dashboard
- View all upcoming games for your active Squad.
- **Voting**: Tap **"JOIN MATCH"** to secure your spot.
- **Waitlist**: If the game is full (usually 14 players), you will be automatically added to the waitlist. If someone leaves, you'll be promoted instantly!

### Payments
- If a game requires a fee, tap **"PAY NOW"** on the game card.
- Select your preferred method (**Zelle** or **PayPal**) and follow the instructions to confirm your payment with the Admin.

## 4. Administrative Features (For Squad Owners)

If you are an Admin, you have access to the **Admin Dashboard**:

### Match Setup
- **Regular Match**: Configure your recurring weekly game (e.g., Every Saturday @ 6 PM). The app will automatically generate these games for you.
- **Custom Polls**: Create one-off events for special tournaments or social gatherings.

### Roster Management
- **Approvals**: See a list of users waiting to join your group and approve them with one tap.
- **Attendance**: View who has joined a match, mark them as paid, or remove them if they can't make it.
